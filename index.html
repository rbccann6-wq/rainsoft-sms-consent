Our SMS Programs

RainSoft of the Wiregrass operates three separate, opt-in SMS communication streams. You will only receive messages from a stream you have explicitly opted into. We do not buy or rent phone lists.

1. Job Applicant Communications

When you apply for a position with RainSoft of the Wiregrass — through our Facebook Lead Ad, by texting our careers number, or by calling our automated hiring agent — you are opting in to receive text messages about your application status, qualifying questions, and interview scheduling.

Hi! 👋 This is the RainSoft of the Wiregrass careers line. We're hiring a Service Admin and I'd love to get you started. First — what's your first and last name? Reply STOP to opt out.

2. Water Test Appointment Confirmations

When you book a free in-home water test or service appointment with RainSoft of the Wiregrass, you are opting in to receive appointment confirmation, reminder, and on-the-way text messages from our team.

RainSoft: Your in-home water test is confirmed for Tue Apr 22 at 2:00 PM. Reply C to confirm, R to reschedule, or STOP to cancel messages.

3. Customer Service Updates

Existing RainSoft customers receive service-related text messages — annual maintenance reminders, technician arrival notices, parts-on-order updates, and warranty notifications. You are opted in by signing your service agreement; you can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

RainSoft Service: Your tech Sarah is on the way and ETA 25 minutes. Reply HELP for help or STOP to opt out of service messages.

How to Opt Out or Get Help

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying with one of the following keywords to any text message we send you:

  • STOP — opt out of all SMS messages
  • UNSUBSCRIBE — same as STOP
  • QUIT — same as STOP
  • CANCEL — same as STOP

After we receive any of those keywords, you will receive one final confirmation that you have been unsubscribed and no further messages will be sent to your number.

To get help, reply HELP to any of our messages, or contact us directly:

  • Email: hiring@rainsoftse.com
  • Phone: (334) 489-1215

Message frequency: varies by program. Hiring conversations typically run 3–10 messages per applicant. Appointment programs send 2–4 messages per appointment. Service updates are infrequent and event-driven.

Carriers: Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Message and data rates may apply.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

What we collect

When you opt in to receive SMS messages from RainSoft of the Wiregrass, we collect: your name, mobile phone number, email address, the program you opted into, and any message content you send to us.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to communicate with you about the SMS program you opted into. Specifically:

  • Job application processing and interview scheduling
  • Water test appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Customer service updates, technician dispatch notifications, and warranty notices
  • Responding to questions you ask us via text

How we share your information

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories of information we collect exclude text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent — this information will not be shared with any third parties under any circumstances.

We use the following service providers to deliver our SMS messages and operate this program: Twilio Inc. (SMS delivery), OpenPhone (number provisioning and inbound routing), and our hiring CRM (applicant tracking). These providers are contractually bound to use your information only to provide the service we have requested.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP
  • Request a copy of the information we have collected about you
  • Request that we delete your information from our systems
  • Update or correct your information

To exercise any of these rights, email hiring@rainsoftse.com or call (334) 489-1215.

Data retention

We retain SMS conversation history and contact information for as long as you remain an active applicant, prospect, or customer, plus a reasonable period after for legal and accounting purposes (typically 7 years). Opt-out records are retained indefinitely so we can honor your opt-out preference.

Children's privacy

Our SMS programs are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this section reflects the latest revision.
